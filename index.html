Story Behind it

At Urban Shift Packers, we prioritize the safety of your valuable LCD/LED screens by using specialized, eco-friendly screen cases instead of traditional wooden crates. Our high-quality protective boxes feature a rigid outer structure with an additional foam layer inside, ensuring double-layer security against external and internal damage during transit. Available in multiple sizes, our customized solutions cater to all LED/LCD models, providing a secure and hassle-free moving experience.

The wooden flooring provides enhanced stability, minimizing jolts and securing goods in place.
